INFERNO

I

THE FIRST REALM

INFERNO

In the beginning, there was only descent. The condemned fall through endless dark before the obsidian plains catch them — vast, cracked, and smoldering with the heat of accumulated sin. Here, in the First Realm, the architecture of damnation expresses itself in towers of petrified agony, bridges of fossilized bone, and rivers of liquid fire that carve channels through mountains of compressed guilt.

The Infernal Circles are not punishment alone — they are record. Every wall bears the imprint of a soul that burned against it. Every iron gate remembers the hand that touched it. The protagonist descends into this realm carrying only a name and a fragment of purpose, and must navigate its labyrinthine floors, where sin has become geography and suffering has been made into a home.

"THE PATH DOWN IS ALWWAYS CLEARER THAN THE PATH UP. THAT IS BY DESIGN"

  • Nine distinct zones of damnation
  • Ancient warden creatures forged from sin
  • Obsidian towers and molten rivers
  • Sealed gates requiring forbidden fire arts

PURGATORY

II

THE SECOND REALM

PURGATORY

Between damnation and ascension lies the twilight country — neither condemned nor redeemed, but waiting. Purgatory is not a punishment; it is a process. Its grey cliffs rise in silent terraces, each carved with the record of a specific human failing. Penitent spirits drift through the ashen winds, whispering half-remembered prayers and fragments of confession.

Time behaves strangely in the Liminal Veil. Memories bleed into physical space — old towers phase in and out of visibility, paths shift between the present and echoes of the past. Navigating Purgatory demands temporal awareness: knowing when to move through memory and when to anchor to the present moment. The protagonist's own forgotten past begins to surface here.

"THE PATH DOWN IS ALWWAYS CLEARER THAN THE PATH UP. THAT IS BY DESIGN"

  • Seven terraces of penitence and reflection
  • Memory echoes as environmental hazards
  • Time-fractured architecture and phasing ruins
  • Temporal movement mechanics unlocked

PARADISE

III

THE THIRD REALM

PARADISE

Radiance is not warmth — it is pressure. Paradise exists above all things, and its light does not illuminate; it reveals. The celestial spires pierce the eternal firmament, arranged in concentric rings of increasing perfection, each governed by an order of guardians who measure worthiness with inhuman precision. Gold and silence define the aesthetic of the highest realm.

Paradise is the most dangerous realm of all. The other planes wore their threats openly — fire and shadow and the weight of time. Here, the danger is subtler: the perfect architecture conceals the most demanding judgment. To ascend to the Empyrean crown, the protagonist must carry every truth earned below without flinching. The final truth of Abyssal waits at the center of the highest ring.

"THE PATH DOWN IS ALWWAYS CLEARER THAN THE PATH UP. THAT IS BY DESIGN"

  • Ten celestial spheres of ascending purity
  • Celestial gatekeepers with divine judgment
  • Gold-and-silence architecture
  • Light-manipulation abilities unlocked
