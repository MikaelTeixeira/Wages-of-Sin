SYSTEMAS & MECHANICS

GAMEPLAY

01

EXPLORATION MECHANICS

Traverse cursed regions filled with hidden paths, forgotten chambers, and ability-gated routes that open as your journey descends deeper into the circles. Every new discovery reshapes the map itself, turning once-hostile spaces into invitations to press further into the unknown.

02

ABILITY PROGRESSION

Shape your build through infernal and sacred powers, unlocking abilities that strengthen combat, alter movement, and reveal new ways to interact with the world. Each choice deepens your identity in battle, allowing different playstyles to emerge from the same descent into darkness.

03

COMBAT SYSTEM

Face relentless enemies in precise, punishing encounters where timing, positioning, and mastery of your abilities decide whether you endure or are consumed. Combat rewards patience as much as aggression, demanding that every strike, dodge, and spell be used with intent.

THE METROIDVANIA SOUL

Abyssal draws on the core pillars of the metroidvania tradition, a living world that evolves with your character's power, where the geography itself is the puzzle. Every room is a sentence; every passage, a paragraph in a story told through stone and shadow. Progression is never granted; it is earned through persistence, discovery, and the courage to descend deeper.

Inspired by the divine structure of Dante's Comedy, the three-act world design mirrors the spiritual arc from damnation to redemption, each plane demanding its own mastery before the next reveals itself.
